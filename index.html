Blood Type Diet Recommendation



Blood Type A - Recommended Diet

Beneficial Foods:

  • Soy products
  • Salmon, sardines
  • Flax & olive oils
  • Broccoli, kale, collards
  • Avocados
  • Berries, green tea

Foods to Avoid:

  • Red meat
  • Full-fat dairy
  • Potatoes
  • Peppers, tomatoes
  • Oranges
  • Processed sugar

Blood Type B - Recommended Diet

Beneficial Foods:

  • Lamb, goat, mutton
  • Cold-water fish
  • Yogurt & kefir
  • Flax & olive oils
  • Garlic & onion
  • Broccoli, kale, turmeric
  • Green tea

Foods to Avoid:

  • Chicken & shellfish
  • Corn & soy products
  • Peanuts, sesame oils
  • Lentils
  • Processed sugar

Blood Type AB - Recommended Diet

Beneficial Foods:

  • Soy products
  • Salmon, sardines
  • Yogurt & kefir
  • Broccoli, cauliflower, tomatoes
  • Garlic & onion
  • Lemon, turmeric
  • Green tea

Foods to Avoid:

  • Chicken
  • Corn products
  • Buckwheat
  • Sesame, sunflower oils
  • Bananas, kidney beans
  • Coffee

Blood Type O - Recommended Diet

Beneficial Foods:

  • Lean, organic, grass-fed meat
  • Cold-water fish
  • Rice & olive oils
  • Garlic & onion
  • Spinach, kale, collards
  • Pineapple, berries
  • Spices like ginger & turmeric

Foods to Avoid:

  • Wheat & wheat products
  • Corn & soy products
  • Cow’s dairy products
  • Potatoes
  • Oranges, kiwis
  • Coffee (even decaf)
  • Processed sugar
